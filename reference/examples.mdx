## Recommended learning path

1. [Introduction](/) — pick a flow and stack under **Integrate by stack**
2. [Quick Start](/quickstart) or a stack guide (Node, Python, PHP, Java, Go, Rust, .NET, Next.js, Laravel)
3. Try endpoints in **API Reference** with your sandbox credentials
4. [Integration guide](/guides/integration) for payout, transfer, refund
